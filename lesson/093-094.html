1 Mr Hill is our new next-door neighbour. 2 He's a pilot. 3 He was in the R. 4 A. 5 F. 6 (皇家空军)He will fly to New York next month. 7 The month after next he will fly to Tokyo. 8 At the moment（现在）, he's in Madrid. 9 He flew to Spain a week ago. 10 He will return to London the week after next. 11 He's only forty-one years old, and he has already been to nearly every country in the world. 12 Mr Hill is a very lucky man. 13 But his wife isn't very lucky. 14 She usually stays at home!
🌐 中文翻译
Mr Hill是我们新搬来的隔壁邻居。 他是个飞行员。 他曾在皇家空军任职。 下个月他将飞往纽约。 再下个月他将飞往东京。 现在他在马德里。 他是一星期以前飞到西班牙的。 再下个星期他将返回伦敦。 他只有41岁，但他却过世界上几乎每一个国家。 Mr Hill是个很幸运的人。 但他的妻子运气不很好。 她总是呆在家里！

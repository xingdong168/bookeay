🎯 Is that you, John?是你吗，约翰？ 1 Mr Grimes: Is that you, John? 2 John Smith: Yes, speaking. 3 Mr Grimes: Tell Mary we shall be late for dinner this evening. 4 John Smith: I'm afraid I don't understand. 5 Mr Grimes: Hasn't Mary told you? 6 She invited betty and me to dinner this evening. 7 I said I would be at your house at six o'clock, but the boss wants me to do some extra work. 8 I'll have to stay at the office. 9 I don't know when I shall finish. 10 Oh, and by the way, my wife wants to know if Mary needs any help. 11 John Smith: I don't know what you're talking about. 12 Mr Grimes: That is John Smith, isn't it? 13 John Smith: Yes, I'm John Smith. 14 Mr Grimes: You are John Smith, the engineer, aren't you? 15 John Smith: That's right. 16 Mr Grimes: You work for the Overseas Engineering Company, don't you? 17 John Smith: No, I don't. 18 I'm John Smith the telephone engineer and I'm repairing your telephone line.

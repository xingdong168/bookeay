For Sale出售​
0:00
← 上一课 下一课 →
1 Mr Hill: Good afternoon. 2 I believe that this house is for sale（待售）. 3 Mr West: That's right. 4 Mr Hill: May I have a look at it please? 5 Mr West: Yes, of course. 6 Come in. 7 Mr Hill: How long have you lived here? 8 Mr West: I have lived here for twenty years. 9 Mr Hill: Twenty years! 10 That's a long time. 11 Mr West: Yes, I have been here since 1947. 12 Mr Hill: Then why do you want to sell it? 13 Mr West: Because I have just retired. 14 I want to buy a small house in the country. 15 Mr Hill: How much does this house cost? 16 Mr West: £6850. 17 Mr Hill: That's a lot of money! 18 Mr West: It's worth every penny of it. 19 Mr Hill: Well, I like the house, but I can't decide yet. 20 My wife must see it first. 21 Mr West: Women always have the last word.
🌐 中文翻译
奈杰尔：下午好。 我想这房子是要出售的吧！ 伊 恩: 是的。 奈杰尔：我可以看一看吗？ 伊 恩: 可以，当然可以。 请进。 奈杰尔：您在这里住了多长时间？ 伊 恩: 我在这里住了20年了。 奈杰恩：20年！ 这个时间可不短。 伊 恩: 是啊，从1976年起我就住在这里。 奈杰尔：那么，您为什么要卖掉它呢？ 伊 恩: 因为我刚退休。 我想在乡下买幢小房子。 奈杰尔: 这座房子卖多少钱？ 伊 恩: 68,500英镑。 奈杰尔：我可真是一大笔钱呢！ 伊 恩: 它确确实实值这么多钱。 奈杰尔：啊，我喜欢这房子，但我还不能决定。 我妻子必须先来看一看。 伊 恩: 女人总是最后说了算的。

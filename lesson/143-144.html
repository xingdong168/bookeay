第 143-144 课
A walk through the woods 林中散步
0:00
← 上一课 下一课 →
1 I live in a very old town which is surrounded by beautiful woods. 2 It is a famous beauty spot. 3 On Sundays, hundreds of people come from the city to see our town and to walk through the woods. 4 Visitors have been asked to keep the woods clean and tidy. 5 Litter baskets have been placed under the trees, but people still throw their rubbish everywhere. 6 Last Wednesday, I went for a walk in the woods. 7 What I saw made me very sad. 8 I counted seven old cars and three old refrigerators. 9 The little baskets were empty and the ground was covered with pieces of paper, cigarette ends, old tyres, empty bottles and rusty tins. 10 Among the rubbish, I found a sign which said, 'Anyone who leaves litter in these woods will be prosecuted! 11 '
🌐 中文翻译
我住在一个由美丽的树林环绕的古老的小镇上。 这是一个著名的风景胜地。 每适星期天，有许许多人从城里来观赏我们的小镇，并在树林中散步。 游客已被告知要保持树林的整洁。 树下都已设置了废物筐，但是人们仍到处扔垃圾。 上星期三我到树林里去散步。 我所见到的一切使我非常难过。 我数了一下，有7辆旧汽车和3个旧冰箱。 废物筐是空的，而满地都是纸片、烟头、旧轮胎，空瓶子和生锈的空罐头盒。 在垃圾堆中我发现了一块牌子，上面写着：“凡在此树林里丢弃垃圾者，将依法处置"。

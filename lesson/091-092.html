1 Mrs Smith: Has Mr West sold his house yet? 2 Mrs Brown: Yes, he has. 3 He sold it last week. 4 Mrs Smith: Has he moved to his new house yet? 5 Mrs Brown: No, not yet. 6 He's still here. 7 He's going to move tomorrow. 8 Mrs Smith: When? 9 Tomorrow morning? 10 Mrs Brown: No. 11 Tomorrow afternoon. 12 I'll miss him. 13 He has always been a good neighbour. 14 Mrs Green: He's a very nice person. 15 We shall all miss him. 16 Mrs Smith; When will the new people move into this house? 17 Mrs Brown: I think that they will move in the day after tomorrow. 18 Mrs Green: Will you see Mr West today, Mrs Brown? 19 Mrs Brown: Yes, I will. 20 Mrs Green: Please give him my regards. 21 （give one's regards to sb. 22 向sb. 23 问候）Mr Smith: Poor Mr West! 24 He didn't want to leave this house. 25 Mrs Brown: No, he didn't want to leave, but his wife did!
🌐 中文翻译
Smith：West先生已把他的房子卖掉了吗？ Brown：是的，卖掉了。 他上星期卖掉的。 Smith：他已经迁进新居了吗？ Brown：不，还没有。 他仍在这里。 他打算明天搬家。 Smith：什么时候？ 明天上午吗？ Brown：不，明天下午。 我会想念他的。 他一直是个好邻居。 Green：他是个非常好的人，我们大学都会想念他的。 Smith：新住户什么时候搬进这所房子？ Brown：我想他们将会在后天搬进来吧。 Green：詹尼，您今天会见到伊恩吗？ Brown：是的，我会见到他。 Green：请代我问候他。 Smith：可怜的West！ 他本不想离开这幢房子。 Brown：是啊，他是不想离开，可是他妻子要离开。

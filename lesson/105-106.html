第 105-106 课
Hello, Mr.boss.您好，老板。
← 上一课 下一课 → ⬇ 音频
1 The Boss: Where's Miss Simpson, Bob? 2 I want her. 3 Bob: Do you want to speak to her, sir? 4 The Boss: Yes, I do. 5 I want her to come to my office. 6 Tell her to come at once. 7 Miss Simpson: Did you want to see me, sir? 8 The Boss: Ah, yes, Miss Simpson. 9 How do you spell“ intelligent”? 10 Can you tell me? 11 Miss Simpson: I-N-T-E-L-L-I-G-E-N-T. 12 The Boss: That's right. 13 You've typed it with only one “L”. 14 This letter's full of mistakes. 15 I want you to type it again. 16 Miss Simpson: Yes, I'll do that. 17 I'm sorry about that. 18 The Boss: And here's a little present for you. 19 Miss Simpson: What is it? 20 The Boss: It's a dictionary. 21 I hope it will help you.
🌐 中文翻译
老 板：鲍勃，Simpson在哪儿？ 我要找她。 鲍 勃：您要同她谈话吗？ 老 板：是的，我要她到我的办公室来。 叫她马上就来。 Simpson：您找我吗？ 老 板：啊，是的，Simpson。 "intelligent" 怎样拼写？ 你能告诉我吗？ Simpson：I-N-T-E-L-L-I-G-E-N-T。 老 板：对的。 但你只打了1个“L”。 这封信里错误百出。 我要你重打一遍。 Simpson：是，我重打。 对此我感到很抱歉。 老 板：这里有一件小礼物送你。 Simpson：是什么？ 老 板：是本词典。 我希望它能对你有所帮助。

mistaken. 错误
presentn. 礼物
dictionaryn. 词典

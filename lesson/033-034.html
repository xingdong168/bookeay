A fine day好天气
← 上一课 下一课 → ⬇ 音频
1 It’s a fine day today. 2 There are some clouds in the sky, but the sun is shining. 3 Mr Jones’s with his family. 4 They are walking over the bridge. 5 There are some boats on the river. 6 Mr Jones and his wife are looking at them. 7 Sally is looking at a big ship. 8 The ship is going under the bridge. 9 Tim is looking at an aeroplane. 10 The aeroplane is flying over the river.
🌐 中文翻译
今天天气好。 天空中飘着几朵云，但阳光灿烂。 琼斯先生同他的家人在一起。 他们正在过桥。 河上有几艘船。 琼斯先生和他的妻子正在看这些船。 莎莉正在观看一艘大船。 那船正从桥下驶过。 蒂姆正望着一架飞机。 飞机正从河上飞过。

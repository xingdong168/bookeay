Uncomfortable shoes不舒服的鞋子

1 LADY : Have you any shoes like these? 2 SALESMAN: What size? 3 Lady: Size five. 4 Salesman: What colour? 5 Lady: Black. 6 Salesman: I’m sorry. 7 We don't have it. 8 Lady: But my sister bought this pair last month. 9 Salesman: Did she buy them here? 10 Lady: No, she bought them in the U. 11 S. 12 Salesman: We had some shoes like those a month ago, but we haven’t any now. 13 Lady: Can you get a pair for me please? 14 Salesman: I’m afraid that I can’t. 15 They were in fashion last year and the year before last. 16 But they’re not in fashion this year. 17 Salesman: These shoes are in fashion now. 18 Lady: They look very uncomfortable. 19 Salesman: They are very uncomfortable. 20 But women always wear uncomfortable shoes!

女 士：像这样的鞋子你们有吗？ 售货员：什么尺码的？ 女 士：5号的。 售货员：什么颜色？ 女 士：黑的售货员：对不起，我们没有。 女 士：但是，我姐姐上个月买到了这样的 一双。 售货员：她是在这儿买的吗？ 女 士：不。 她是在美国买的。 售货员：一个月前我们有这要的鞋。 但是现在没有了。 女 士：您能为我找一双吗？ 售货员：恐怕不行。 这鞋在去年和前年时兴，而今年已不流行了。 售货员：现在流行的是这种鞋子。 女 士：这种鞋子看上去很不舒适。 售货员：的确很不舒适。 可是女人们总是穿不舒适的鞋子！

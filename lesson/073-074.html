The way to King Street通往国王街的路
0:00
← 上一课 下一课 →
1 Last week Mrs Mills went to London. 2 She does not know London very well, and she lost her way. 3 Suddenly, she saw a man near a bus-stop. 4 “ I can ask him the way. 5 ” She said to herself. 6 “Excuse me,” she said. 7 “Can you tell me the way to King Street please? 8 ”The man smiled pleasantly. 9 He did not understand English! 10 He spoke German. 11 He was a tourist. 12 Then he put his hand into his pocket, and took out a phrase-book. 13 He opened the book and found a phrase. 14 He read the phrase slowly. 15 “I am sorry,” he said. 16 “ I do not speak English. 17 ”
🌐 中文翻译
上星期米尔斯夫人去了伦敦。 她对伦敦不很熟悉，因此迷了路。 突然，她在公共汽车站附近看到一个男人。 “我可以向他问路，”她想。 “对不起，您能告诉我到国王街怎么走吗？ ”她说。 这人友好地笑了笑。 他不懂英语！ 他讲德语。 他是个旅游者。 然后他把手伸进了衣袋，掏出了一本常用语手册。 他翻开书找到了一条短语。 他缓慢地读着短语。 ”很抱歉，“他说，”我不会讲英语。 ”

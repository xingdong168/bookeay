1 George: Hullo, Ken. 2 Ken: Hullo, George. 3 George: Have you just been to the cinema? 4 Ken: Yes, I have. 5 George: What's on? 6 Ken: “Paris in the Spring”. 7 George: Oh, I've already seen it. 8 I saw it on a B. 9 B. 10 C. 11 television programme last year. 12 It's an old film, but it's very good. 13 Ken: Paris is a beautiful city. 14 George: I have never been there. 15 Have you ever been there, Ken? 16 Ken: Yes, I have. 17 I was there in April. 18 George: Paris in the spring, eh? 19 Ken: It was spring, but the weather was awful. 20 It rained all the time. 21 George: Just like dear old London!
🌐 中文翻译
乔治：你好，肯。 肯：你好，乔治。 乔治：你刚去过电影院吗？ 肯：是的，我刚去过。 乔治：上映什么片子？ 肯：《巴黎之春》。 乔治：噢，我已看过了。 我是去年在电视上看的。 这是老部片子，但很好。 肯：巴黎是座美丽的城市。 乔治：我从未去过。 肯，你去过吗？ 肯：是的，我去过。 4月份我在那儿。 乔治：那是巴黎之春，是吗？ 肯：是春天，但天气太糟了。 一直在下雨。 乔治：就像伦敦一样！

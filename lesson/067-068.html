The weekend周末
← 上一课 下一课 → ⬇ 音频
1 MRS JOHNSON: Hello. 2 Were you at the butcher's? 3 MRS WILLIAMS: Yes. 4 I was. 5 Were you at the butcher's ,too? 6 MRS JOHNSON: No, I wasn't. 7 I was at the greengrocer's. 8 How's Jimmy today? 9 MRS WILLIAMS: He's very well, thank you. 10 MRS JOHNSON: Was he absent from school last week? 11 MRS WILLIAMS: Yes, he was. 12 He was absent on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday. 13 How are you all keeping? 14 MRS JOHNSON: Very well, thank you. 15 We’re going to spend three days in the country. 16 We’re going to stay at my mother's for the week-end. 17 MRS WILLIAMS: Friday, Saturday and Sunday in the country! 18 Aren't you lucky!
🌐 中文翻译
约翰逊夫人：您好。 刚才您在肉店里吗？ 威廉斯夫人：是的，我在肉店里。 您也在肉店里吗？ 约翰逊夫人：不， 我不是。 我在蔬菜水果店里。 吉米今天怎么样？ 威廉斯夫人：他很好，谢谢您。 约翰逊夫人：上星期他没上学吧？ 威廉斯夫人：是的，他没上学。 他星期一、星期二、星期三和星期四没去上学。 你们身体都好吗？ 约翰逊夫人：很好，谢谢您。 我们打算到乡下去三三，在我母亲家度周末。 威廉斯夫人：星期五、星期六和星期日在乡下过！ 你们真幸运啊！

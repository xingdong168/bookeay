🎯 A good idea好主意 1 Betty: Shall I make some coffee, Jane? 2 Jane: That's a good idea, Betty. 3 Betty: It's ready. 4 Do you want any milk? 5 Jane: Just a little please. 6 Betty: What about some sugar? 7 Two teaspoonfuls? 8 Jane: No, less than that. 9 One and a half teaspoonfuls please. 10 That's enough for me. 11 Jane: That was very nice. 12 Betty: Would you like some more? 13 Jane: Yes, please. 14 Jane: I'd like a cigarette, too. 15 May I have one? 16 Betty: Of course. 17 I think there are a few in that box. 18 Jane: I'm afraid it's empty. 19 Betty: What a pity! 20 （真遗憾）Jane: It doesn't matter. 21 Betty: Have a biscuit instead. 22 Eat more and smoke less! 23 Jane: That's very good advice!
🌐 中文翻译
夏洛特：我来煮点咖啡好吗，简？ 简：这是个好主意，夏洛特。 夏洛特：咖啡好了，你要放点奶吗？ 简：请稍加一点。 夏洛特：加些糖怎么样？ 两茶匙行吗？ 简：不，再少一些。 请放一勺儿半。 那对我已足够了。 简：太好了。 夏洛特：你再来点吗？ 简：好的，请再来一点。 简：我还想抽枝烟。 可以给我一枝吗？ 夏洛特：当然可以。 我想那个盒子里有一些。 简：恐怕盒子是空的。 夏洛特：真遗憾！ 简：没关夏洛特：那就吃块饼干吧。 多吃点，少抽点！ 简：这是极好的忠告啊！

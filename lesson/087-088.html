A car crash车祸

1 Mr Wood: Is my car ready yet? 2 Attendant: I don't know, sir. 3 What's the licence number of your car? 4 Mr Wood: It's LFZ 312 G. 5 Attendant: When did you bring it to us? 6 Mr Wood: I brought it here three days ago. 7 Attendant: Ah yes, I remember now. 8 Mr Wood: Have your mechanics finished yet? 9 Attendant: No, they're still working on it. 10 Let's go into the garage and have a look at it. 11 Attendant: Isn't that your car? 12 Mr Wood: Well, it was my car. 13 Attendant: Didn't you have a crash? 14 Mr Wood: That's right. 15 She drove it into a lamp post（电线杆）. 16 Can your mechanics repair it? 17 Attendant: Well, they're trying to repair it, sir. 18 But to tell you the truth, you need a new car.
🌐 中文翻译
伍德先生：我的汽车修好了吗？ 服 务 员：我不知道，先生。 您的汽车牌号是多少？ 伍德先生：是 LFZ312G。 服 务 员：您什么时候送来的？ 伍德先生：3天前。 服 务 员：啊，是的，我现在记起来了。 伍德先生：你们的机械师修好了吗？ 服 务 员：没有，他们还在修呢。 我们到车库去看一下吧。 服 务 员：这难道不是您的车吗？ 伍德先生：唔，这曾是我的车。 服 务 员：难说您没有出车祸吗？ 伍德先生：是啊。 我把汽车撞在电线杆上了。 你们的机械师能修好吗？ 服 务 员：啊，他们正设法修呢，先生。 不过说实在的，您需要一辆新车了。

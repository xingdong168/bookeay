1 BUTCHER: Do you want any meat today. 2 Mrs. Bird? 3 MRS. 4 BIRD: Yes, please. 5 BUTCHER: Do you want beef or lamb? 6 MRS. 7 BIRD: Beef, please. 8 BUTCHER: This lamb's very good. 9 MRS. 10 BIRD: I like lamb, but my husband doesn't. 11 BUTCHER: What about some steak? 12 This is a nice piece. 13 MRS. 14 BIRD: Give me that piece, please. 15 MRS. 16 BIRD: And a pound of mince, too. 17 BUTCHER: Do you want a chicken, Mrs. Bird? 18 They 're very nice. 19 MRS. 20 BIRD: No, thank you. 21 MRS. 22 BIRD: My husband likes steak, but he doesn't like chicken. 23 BUTCHER: To tell you the truth, Mrs. Bird, I don't like chicken either!
🌐 中文翻译
肉 商：您今天要买点肉吗，伯德夫人？ 伯德夫人：是的，我买一点。 肉 商：您要牛肉还是要羔羊肉？ 伯德夫人：请给我牛肉。 肉 商：这羔羊肉很好。 伯德夫人：我喜欢羔羊肉，可我丈夫不喜欢。 肉 商：来点牛排吗？ 这块很好。 伯德夫人：就请给我那块吧。 伯德夫人：再来一磅绞肉。 肉 商：您要买只鸡吗，伯德夫人？ 这些鸡很好。 伯德夫人：不要了，谢谢。 伯德夫人：我丈夫喜欢牛排，但他不喜欢鸡。 肉 商：说老实话，伯德夫人，我也不喜欢鸡。

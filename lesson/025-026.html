🎯 Mrs. Smith’s kitchen史密斯太太的厨房 1 Mrs. Smith’s kitchen is small. 2 There is a refrigerator in the kitchen. 3 The refrigerator is white. 4 It is on the right. 5 There is an electric cooker in the kitchen. 6 The cooker is blue. 7 It is on the left. 8 There is a table in the middle of the room. 9 There is a bottle on the table. 10 The bottle is empty. 11 There is a cup on the table, too. 12 The cup is clean.
🌐 中文翻译
史密斯夫人的厨房很小。 厨房里有个电冰箱。 冰箱的颜色是白的。 它位于房间右侧。 厨房里有个电灶。 电灶的颜色是蓝的。 它位于房间左侧。 房间的中央有张桌子。 桌子上有个瓶子。 瓶子是空的。 桌子上还有一只杯子。 杯子很干净。

The man in the hat戴帽子的男人
← 上一课 下一课 → ⬇ 音频
1 Customer: I bought two expensive dictionaries here half an hour ago, but I forgot to take them with me. 2 Manager: Who served you, sir? 3 Customer: The lady who is standing behind the counter. 4 Manager: Which books did you buy? 5 Customer: The books which are on the counter. 6 Manager: Did you serve this gentleman half an hour ago, Miss Roberts? 7 He says he's the man who bought these books. 8 Miss Roberts: I can't remember, sir. 9 The man whom I served was wearing a hat. 10 Manager: Have you got a hat, sir? 11 Customer: Yes, I have. 12 Manager: Would you put it on, please? 13 Customer: All right. 14 Manager: Is this the man that you served, Miss Roberts? 15 Miss Roberts: Yes, sir. 16 I recognize him now.
🌐 中文翻译
顾 客：半小时以前我在这里买了两本很贵的辞典，但是我忘了拿走。 经 理：是谁接待您的，先生？ 顾 客：站在柜台后面的那位女士。 经 理：您买的是两本什么书？ 顾 客：就是柜台上的那两本。 经 理：卡罗琳，半小时前你接待过这位先生吗？ 他说他就是买这两本书的人。 卡罗琳：我记不起来了。 我接待的那个人戴着一顶帽子。 经 理：先生，您有帽子吗？ 顾 客：有的，我有帽子。 经 理：请您把帽子戴上好吗？ 顾 客：好吧。 经 理：卡罗琳，这就是你接待过的那个人吗？ 卡罗琳：是他。 我现在认出他来了。

A card from Jimmy吉米的来信

1 Grandmother: Read Jimmy's card to me please, Mary. 2 Mary: “I have just arrived in Scotland and I'm staying at a Youth Hostel. 3 ”Grandmother: Eh? 4 Mary: He says he's just arrived in Scotland. 5 He says he's staying at a Youth Hostel. 6 You know he's a member of the Y. 7 H. 8 A. 9 Grandmother: The what? 10 Mary: The Y. 11 H. 12 A. 13 , mother. 14 The Youth Hostel's Association. 15 Grandmother: What else does he say? 16 Mary: “I'll write a letter soon. 17 I hope you are all well. 18 ”Grandmother: What? 19 Speak up, Mary. 20 I'm afraid I can't hear you. 21 Mary: He says he'll write a letter soon. 22 He hopes we are all well. 23 “ Love, Jimmy. 24 ”Grandmother: Is that all? 25 He doesn't say very much, does he? 26 Mary: He can't write very much on a card, mother.
🌐 中文翻译
祖母：请把吉米的明信片念给我听听，Mary。 Mary：“我刚到苏格兰，我现住在一家青年招待所。 ”祖母：什么？ Mary：他说他刚到苏格兰。 他说他住在一家青年招待所。 你知道，他是“青招协” 的一个成员。 祖母：什么？ Mary：“青招协”，妈妈。 青年招待所协会。 祖母：他还说了些什么？ Mary：“我很快会写信的。 祝你们大家身体都好。 ”祖母：什么？ Mary，大声一点。 我可听不见你念的。 Mary：他说他很快会写信的。 他祝我们大家身体好。 “谨此问候，吉米。 ”祖母：就这些吗？ 他没写许多，是吗？ Mary：在明信片上他写不了很多，妈妈。

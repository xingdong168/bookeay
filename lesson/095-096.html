1 George: Two return tickets to London please. 2 What time will the next train leave? 3 Attendant: At nineteen minutes past eight. 4 George: Which platform? 5 Attendant: Platform Two. 6 Over the bridge. 7 Ken: What time will the next train leave? 8 George: At eight nineteen. 9 Ken: We've got plenty of time. 10 George: It's only three minutes to eight. 11 Ken: Let's go and have a drink. 12 There's a bar next door to the station. 13 George: We had better go back to the station now, Ken. 14 Porter: Tickets please. 15 George: We want to catch the eight nineteen to London. 16 Porter: You've just missed it! 17 George: What! 18 It's only eight fifteen. 19 Porter: I'm sorry, sir. 20 That clock's ten minutes slow. 21 George: When's the next train? 22 Porter: In five hours' time!
